About me

I am Richard Oguzie-Ibeh from Nigeria, I studied Mechatronics Engineering in the Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO), Over there I learnt a lot about robotics and automation, I have always wanted to be the best I could be, so I decided to know more about other related fields like computer science where I got to understand the working principles of Artificial Intelligence from something as simple as a perceptron to complex layers of neural networks all connected to be trained and tested.

After the exciting moments of gaining knowledge, I felt happy but not satisfied, I wanted something more than knowledge, I wanted to build something, I started with android development where I used a Google frame work (FLUTTER Tool Kit) to build mobile applications for andriod and IOS, within that period I learnt web development and dived into the software world, now I know who I am.
